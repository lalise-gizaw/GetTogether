Project Description

GetTogether is a web-based platform designed to foster real-world connections through shared hobbies and interests. Whether you’re new to a city, a student looking to meet peers, or just hoping to socialize around activities you enjoy, GetTogether makes it easy to discover and join local events. Users can browse a personalized event feed, create their own events with details like time, location, and description, and connect with others through profile pages and integrated chat. The platform emphasizes simplicity and accessibility, offering smart filters to tailor the experience to each user’s preferences. Profile pages highlight bios, hobbies, and friends to build trust and encourage social discovery. GetTogether turns the idea of “we should hang out sometime” into actual meetups—with just a few clicks.
